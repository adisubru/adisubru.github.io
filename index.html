I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Automation at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Arindam Khan. Prior to this I did my Bachelor’s from Shiv Nadar University, Noida, and am grateful for the guidance of Prof. Sandeep Sen for my undergraduate project. I am a recipient of KIAC (Kotak AI/ML Center) Fellowship, and the Walmart Fellowship.

My interests lie in the general area of Theoretical Computer Science and I am currently working on finding approximation algorithms for geometric optimization problems, and on some online network design problems. I enjoy thinking about problems in graph algorithms and combinatorial optimization, and look forward to exploring more of TCS.

Outside of TCS, I spend a lot of time speedcubing, trekking, and am also taking training in the Indian percussion instrument - Tabla, under the tutelage of Sri. Amit Mishra of the Banaras Gharana. Lastly, since you didn’t ask, I use arch btw. You can find out more at my official WCA page and my dotfiles repo.
